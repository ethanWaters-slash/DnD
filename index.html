Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2023  ·  Last Updated: 2025

🔒

D&D Character Companion does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — ever. Your characters belong to you alone.


I. Data Collection

This app collects no personal information whatsoever. We do not collect your name, email address, device identifiers, location, usage analytics, or any other data of any kind.

II. Local Storage

All character data you create within the app is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server, database, or third party.

III. Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain advertising-related data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. This is limited to standard ad-serving functionality. The developer of this app receives no personal data from these interactions.

IV. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. As no data is collected from any user, this app is safe for all age groups in that regard.

V. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the new version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

VI. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue or reach out via the GitHub repository.
